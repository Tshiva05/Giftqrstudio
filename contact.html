Frequently Asked Questions

How long are my uploaded files stored?

Files are stored on Cloudinary indefinitely unless you request removal. QR codes do not expire, but if you want a file deleted, contact us with the URL.

Is GiftQR Studio really free?

Yes, completely free. The service is supported by advertisements. No account or payment is required.

Can I upload any file type?

We support images (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP), videos (MP4, WebM, MOV) and audio (MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A). Maximum sizes are 10 MB for images, 50 MB for videos and 20 MB for audio.

Who can see my uploaded gift?

Anyone with the unique QR link or URL can view the gift. We do not publish a public gallery of uploads. Only share your QR with people you trust.
