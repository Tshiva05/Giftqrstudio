Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · We keep it simple and honest.

Overview

GiftQR Studio ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using our website at gift-qr-studio.onrender.com, you agree to this policy.

Files You Upload

When you upload a photo, video or audio file to create a gift QR, that file is:

  • Temporarily stored on our server and then uploaded to Cloudinary, our secure cloud storage provider.
  • The temporary local copy is immediately deleted after the Cloudinary upload completes.
  • Stored on Cloudinary under the folder giftqr_studio associated with our account.
  • Accessible to anyone who has the unique QR link — please only share links with people you trust.

We do not actively monitor or view your uploaded files. However, we reserve the right to remove content that violates our terms (e.g. illegal content, explicit material).

Cookies & Tracking

We currently use no tracking cookies of our own. However:

  • Google AdSense (when enabled) may set advertising cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Fonts and Font Awesome CDN may log your IP address for performance purposes.

Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit and use GiftQR Studio. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally. No names, emails or personal details are collected.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Cloudinary – file storage. cloudinary.com/privacy
  • Render – website hosting. render.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense – advertising (when active). policies.google.com/privacy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any file you uploaded (contact us with the file URL).
  • Know what data we hold about you (we hold very little — no accounts exist).
  • Opt out of advertising personalisation via Google Ad Settings.

Children's Privacy

GiftQR Studio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has uploaded content, please contact us immediately and we will remove it.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us here and we'll respond within 48 hours.

Disclaimer: GiftQR Studio is a personal/small-business project. Uploaded files are user-generated content. We are not responsible for the content of shared gift links. Please use the service responsibly and legally.
